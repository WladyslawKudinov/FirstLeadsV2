## Value Equation Analysis

| Lever | Current State | Our Offer | Score |
|---|---|---|---|
| Dream Outcome | Lawsuits defended with photos + lawyers (20-50% commission) | Video evidence of every apartment → cases dismissed or prevented | 8/10 |
| Perceived Likelihood | No pilot in acceptance. No proof video > photo in court. | Blue ocean — no one offers video. But hypothesis, not fact. | 5/10 |
| Time to Result | Current platforms (Базис, Домиленд) already deployed | Add video layer in 1 day on top of existing workflow | 8/10 |
| Effort & Sacrifice | "We'd need to change our acceptance process" | Zero changes. Inspector walks through apartment with camera. | 9/10 |

**Overall Value Score: 7.5/10** — Good potential, but weakened by lack of proof. The 5/10 on likelihood is the critical gap. Compensate with: (1) free pilot, (2) blue ocean framing, (3) moratorium urgency.

**CRITICAL CAVEAT:** 73% of lawsuits = delays, only 18% = quality defects. Video helps with 18%. Be transparent about this in all materials. Don't oversell.

## A/B Testing Plan

### Week 1

| Test | Variant A | Variant B | Metric | Decision Rule |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Subject line | «Видеоприёмка квартир — то, чего нет ни у кого» | «Мораторий отменён — 100 000 исков на 200 млрд ₽» | Open rate | Winner > 25% |
| Offer body | Variant A (result/blue ocean) | Variant B (pain/moratorium) | Reply rate | Winner > 3% |
| CTA | «15 минут показать?» | «Стоит 15 минут обсудить?» | Call bookings | Winner = more calls |

**Volume:** 30 emails per variant (60 total)
**Segment:** Девелоперы — приёмка квартир — 5 top targets + extended list
**Timeline:** 5 business days
**Target companies (week 1):** DOGMA, ГК ССК, GloraX, ГК ФСК, Брусника + next 25 from исковая нагрузка table

### Week 2

- Scale winning variant
- Cold calls to openers who didn't reply
- LinkedIn outreach to quality directors
- **Parallel:** 2-3 interviews with developer lawyers to validate "video > photo in court" hypothesis

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Action if below |
|---|---|---|
| Open rate | >25% | Test moratorium-focused subjects |
| Reply rate | >3% | Shift from blue-ocean to fear-based messaging |
| Call bookings | >3 in week 1 | Add cold calls earlier in sequence |
| Qualified leads | >1 in 2 weeks | If zero — reassess: is video acceptance a real need? |

### Special Note: Hypothesis Validation

This segment has a critical unvalidated hypothesis: **video > photo in court for apartment defect claims.**

If week 1 reply rate is near zero, it may mean:
1. The hypothesis is wrong (photo is "enough")
2. The moratorium timing is off (DMs not worried yet)
3. The DM target is wrong (should target legal, not quality)

**Decision point at end of week 1:** If <2% reply rate across both variants, pivot to interviewing developer lawyers before continuing outreach.
