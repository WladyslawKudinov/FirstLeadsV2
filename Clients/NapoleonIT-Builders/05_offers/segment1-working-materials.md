## A/B Testing Plan

### Week 1

| Test | Variant A | Variant B | Metric | Decision Rule |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Subject line | «Споры с подрядчиками: 5 минут вместо 5 недель» | «Сколько стоит один спор с подрядчиком?» | Open rate | Winner > 25% |
| Offer body | Variant A (result) | Variant B (pain) | Reply rate | Winner > 3% |
| CTA | «15 минут — покажу, как это выглядит?» | «Есть 15 минут обсудить?» | Call bookings | Winner = more calls |

**Volume:** 30 emails per variant (60 total) — limited by segment size (~68 companies)
**Segment:** Стройконтроль генподрядчиков — 68+ companies identified
**Timeline:** 5 business days
**Target companies (week 1):** Монолит, Брусника, МСУ-1, Setl Group, ГК ТОЧНО + next 25 from extended list

### Week 2

- Scale winning variant to remaining companies
- Cold calls to all who opened but didn't reply (Day 8-10)
- Variant C (simplicity) as follow-up pivot for non-responders
- LinkedIn outreach to DMs who didn't engage via email

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Action if below |
|---|---|---|
| Open rate | >25% | Change subject lines |
| Reply rate | >3% | Change offer body / value proposition |
| Call bookings | >5 in week 1 | Change CTA / add phone follow-up |
| Qualified leads | >2 in 2 weeks | Reassess segment fit |
